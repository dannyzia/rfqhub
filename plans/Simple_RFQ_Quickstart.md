# Simple RFQ Quickstart

## Required Fields
- Buyer contact info
- Item list (max 20 items)
- Delivery terms
- Basic commercial terms

## Auto-Publish Rules:
1. Goes live immediately after creation
2. Minimum open period: 24 hours
3. Automatic bid ranking by price

## Limitations:
- No complex evaluation criteria
- Maximum 5 attached documents
- No Bangladesh e-GP compliance checks
