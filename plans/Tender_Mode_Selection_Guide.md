# Tender Mode Selection Guide

## Modes Available
1. **Simple RFQ** - For quick commercial purchases (<$50k)
2. **Detailed RFT** - Full government compliance (Bangladesh e-GP types)
3. **Live Auction** - Real-time bidding with vendor pre-selection

## Selection Criteria
| Factor            | Simple RFQ | Detailed RFT | Live Auction |
|-------------------|------------|--------------|--------------|
| Procurement Value | <$50k      | Any          | >$100k       |
| Complexity        | Low        | High         | Medium       |
| Compliance Needs  | Minimal    | Full e-GP    | Custom       |
